Challenge: Rocket Launch Timeline

Create a rocket launch sequence using gsap.timeline(). The challenge is to orchestrate multiple animations in perfect synchronization to simulate a realistic rocket launch.

Your timeline should include: countdown animation, smoke effects, rocket lift-off, flame ignition, and background elements reacting to the launch.

Use timeline features like position parameters, labels, and relative timing to create smooth, overlapping animations that feel natural and exciting.

Bonus: Add interactive controls to play, pause, reverse, and restart the launch sequence!
